    TOP REASONS WHY IT WAS FAKE:

    #1. No Wind


    On the moon and in all of space there is no air or wind, since space is a vacuum the flag shouldn't be flapping in the wind like it is in many of the videos and images.







    #2 No Stars

    In space there are many stars and galaxies afar. However, due to our polluted and smoggy atmosphere it can be hard to see these at night. In space and on the moon there should be many visible stars. However, in many of the photos there are no stars in the sky and the vacuum of space looks like a black screen.






    #3. Visor Reflection

    It's rummored to believe that there are cameramen visible in the visor screen of the astronauts in some of the original pictures
